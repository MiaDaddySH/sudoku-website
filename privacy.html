Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users. The only data collection service we use is AdMob.

AdMob

We use AdMob to serve advertisements in our application. AdMob may collect and use data as described in its privacy policy. The types of data collected by AdMob include:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Log information (e.g., IP address, crash reports)
  • Location information (e.g., coarse location based on network)
  • Ad performance data (e.g., interactions with ads)

For more information on how AdMob collects and uses data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.
